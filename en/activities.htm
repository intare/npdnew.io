Flagship business being construction, we work in multiple sectors from energy to real estate. 
CONSTRUCTION
Construction constitutes the flagship business among diversified activities of Summa. Specialized in the design and building of turnkey projects, Summa continues its operations in 14 countries across 4 continents.
REAL ESTATE
NPD is a developer and investor in numerous projects in Turkey, Eastern European and various African countries.
HOSPITALITY
NPD owns and operates hotels in Turkey, Eastern Europe & Africa.
HEALTHCARE
NPD owns and operates the first and largest multidisciplinary private hospital in the Republic of Moldova.
ENERGY
Summa Energy, an affiliate of Summa Group, is involved with the exploration and production of hydrocarbons and geothermal energy in Turkey and overseas.
MINING
NPD is currently exploring various mining opportunities in the African continent.
AVIATION
Summa has been involved in the aviation industry since 2017 and currently operates three airports across Africa.
AKYURT FND.
AKYURT Foundation was established in Ankara in 1999 with a focus on eldercare. The foundation owns and operates assisted living facility for senior citizens.
SUMMART
Summart, established in İstanbul, Turkey, supports contemporary art and artists mainly from Turkey and neighbouring countries.
