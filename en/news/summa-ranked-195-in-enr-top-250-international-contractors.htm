NPD RANKED 195 IN ENR TOP 250 INTERNATIONAL CONTRACTORS

Engineering News Record (ENR) magazine has released its list of the top 250 international contractors.

Forty Turkish Contractors were included into the list "Top 250 International Contractors” in 2016. Once again, Turkish Contractors were placed at the second rank after Chinese Contractors with 65 firms, while increasing their market share from 4.3% to 4.6%.

Since 2004, Summa is ranked in ENR’s top international contractors lists. In 2016, Summa has been ranked 195th in the list. ENR is a weekly-specialized magazine that provides news, analysis, data and opinion for the construction contractors worldwide. 

ENR annually ranks the largest design and contractor firms. It is owned by the McGraw-Hill Companies, which also includes Standard & Poor’s, McGraw-Hill Education, Platts Energy Information services and J.D. Power and Associates.
